Termfolio is a terminal-inspired Hugo theme for people who want writing, projects, and metadata to feel like a browsable repository. It bundles its font locally, avoids tracking, and keeps the rendered site useful without depending on a client-side app.

{{< figure src="example.svg" alt="A simple local landscape illustration" caption="A local page-bundle image rendered without external requests." >}}

The theme is intentionally plain, but not unfinished. Posts get readable spacing, accessible focus states, optional contents lists, tidy metadata, local Open Graph metadata, and code styles tuned for the dark terminal palette.

It should feel quick to understand, quick to load, and easy to adapt.
